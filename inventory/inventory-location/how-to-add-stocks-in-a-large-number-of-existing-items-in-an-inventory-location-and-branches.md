# How to add stocks in a large number of existing items in an inventory location and branches?

## <mark style="color:orange;">**How to add stocks**</mark>

1. Click on Inventory

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Click on Inventory locations.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

2. Click on Inventory locations

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Click on Inventory locations.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

3. Choose the inventory location where you want to add stocks.

{% hint style="info" %}
If you want to add to your stock in branches, go to stores/branches.
{% endhint %}

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Choose the inventory location where you want to add stocks..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

4. Click on Stores / Branches

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Click on Stores Branches.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

5. Similarly, under the inventory location, choose the store/branch where you want to add stocks.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Similarly, under the inventory location, choose the storebranch where you want to add stocks..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

6. Getting back to the inventory location, you will see a "add stock" button on the right side of the top.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Getting back to the inventory location, you will see a add stock button on the right side of the top..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

7. Click on Add stocks

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Click on Add stocks.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

8. This is the detail page for adding stocks. You may also download an example in csv format for reference.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/This is the detail page for adding stocks. You may also download an example in csv format for reference..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

9. Click on Next Step

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Click on Next Step.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

10. Click to choose a file or drag and drop here.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Click to choose a file or drag and drop here..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

11. These are the items that I intend to upload to my chosen location. \*GlutaGen Z - 20 pcs \*Apple Cider - 20 pcs \*Collagen - 0 pc \*Frozen collagen - 2 pcs

Please keep in mind that zero stocks will not be kept there. Only existing inventory items will be added, and the current and new additional quantities will be combined.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/These are the items that I intend to upload to my chosen location..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

12. As you can see from my inventory items, GlutaGen Z, Apple Cider, and Collagen already exist. Frozen collagen is not present, and it will be rejected once uploaded.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/As you can see from my inventory items, GlutaGen Z, Apple Cider, and Collagen already exist. Frozen collagen is not present, and it will be rejected once uploaded..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

13. After you've added the csv file, click the next step button.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/After you&#x27;ve added the csv file, click the next step button..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

14. If you see these two checked in green highlighted words, it means you can continue on to the next phase.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/f you see this two checked in green highlighted words, it means you can continue on to the next phase..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

15. Click on Import Data

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Click on Import Data.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

16. Please wait while your data is imported.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Please wait while your data is imported..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

17. As you can see, just three products were imported because the other one was not registered.

{% hint style="info" %}
Note: You may also download the rejected items as well as the imported items if you choose.
{% endhint %}



<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/As you can see, just three products were imported because the other one was not registered..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

18. To finish, click on the close button.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/To finish, click on the close button..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
