# How to Deplete items quantity?

## <mark style="color:orange;">**Deplete item stocks**</mark>

1. Click on Inventory

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Click on Inventory.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

2. This is the inventory item page.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/This is the inventory item page..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

3. Select the item you want to deplete.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Select the item you want to deplete..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

4. This is the item details page.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/.This is the item details page..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

5. Choose the stocks by lot items that correspond to the location or branch you would like to deplete.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Choose the stocks by lot items that correspond to the location or branch you would like to deplete..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

6. Click on Deplete

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Click on Deplete.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

7. Enter the quantity that needs to be removed from the stock of lot items.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Enter the quantity that needs to be removed from the stock of lot items..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

8. Click on Submit

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Click on Submit.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

9. Item successfully depleted

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Item successfully depleted.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

10. As you can see, the stocks of chosen lot items have already been depleted, and there is also a history record on the right side.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/As you can see, the stocks of chosen lot items have already been depleted, and there is also a history record on the right side..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
