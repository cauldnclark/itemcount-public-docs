# How to transfer item(s) by Branch and by Inventory Location?



## <mark style="color:orange;">How to transfer item(s) by Branch and by Inventory Location?</mark>



**Branch to location and vice versa**

**Change the location of a single item.**

1. Open the inventory list and choose the items you want to be relocated from the branch or location.

{% hint style="info" %}
Note: Check to see if there's a stock of the item before moving on to the next step.
{% endhint %}

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Open the inventory list and choose the items you want to be relocated from the branch or location..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

2. To display the item's quantity, click the down arrow.

{% hint style="info" %}
Note: The stock count for that item is indicated by the number next to its name.
{% endhint %}

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/To display the item&#x27;s quantity, click the down arrow..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

3. Select "transfer"

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Select transfer.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

4. Details of item transfer.

{% hint style="info" %}
Note: When transferring locations, the item's name cannot be edited.
{% endhint %}

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Details of item transfer..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

5. Add Quantity

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Add Quantity.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

6. After adding the quantity, click the down arrow inside "To type" to show which location or branch you want to transfer to.

{% hint style="info" %}
Note: You may also transfer item quantities from branch to branch; it is up to you whatever type you would like to transfer.
{% endhint %}

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/After adding the quantity, click the down arrow inside To type to show which location or branch you want to transfer to..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



7. After picking a "type" (branch, location, or reject), the opposing side will show on what you choose.

{% hint style="info" %}
Note: If you click the down arrow on the other side, the proposed branches or location will show immediately.
{% endhint %}

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/After picking a typebranchlocationor reject, the opposing side will show on what you choose..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

8. An example view if you click the location type.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/An example view if you click the location type..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

9. Receiving by is optional. This is just evidence of your transaction.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Receiving by is optional. This is just evidence of your transaction..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

10. Click the submit button.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Click the submit button..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

11. The item was successfully transferred.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/The item was successfully transferred..png" alt="" width="484"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

12. On the item details page, you can see the transaction history.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/On the item details page, you can see the transaction history..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
