# How to Apply Senior Discount & Set Item as Free

1. Let's start with your cart view.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/1-cart display (1).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

2. Click the item you want to discount.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/2-Click the item you have chosen to apply the discount to..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

3. Click on 'Discount Privilege'.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/3-Click on Discount Privilege.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

4. Select the desired discount privilege.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/4-Choose on Discount Privilege you wanted to apply on specific item.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

5. Click 'SENIOR (20%)'.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/5-Click on SENIOR (20%).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

6. Click 'Update' to apply the discount.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/6-After selecting discount, Click on Update.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

7. Your item is now discounted.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/7-chosen item has already been discounted.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

8. Click the item you want to set as free.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/8-click on item you want to set as freebie.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

9. Check 'Set as Free' and click 'Update'.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/9-Check SET AS FREE.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

10. Click 'Charge', fill in customer info.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/11-Click on Charge.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

11. **Fill in the customers name and ID number**

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/12-fill in the customers name and ID number.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

12. Click 'Next' to proceed to payment.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/14-Click on Next to proceed in payment.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

13. Select the payment method and proceed.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/16-You have the option to select the payment method that they prefer..png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

14. Transaction complete. Click 'Complete' to return to the POS display.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/18-transaction complete.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
